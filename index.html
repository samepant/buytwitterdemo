Twitter is a vital utility for news, media, and social movements.

But with stock prices lower than ever, we as users and shareholders could lose everything.

That’s why we are proposing Twitter commission a study of ways to increase its value, and protect the platform, through models of democratic ownership.

We believe Twitter needs better governance like REI, Associated Press, and other successful enterprises that effectively engage over one billion members worldwide.

ARE YOU A SHAREHOLDER?

Our proposal is up for a vote!

Our proposal goes up for a vote now until May 22. We need 50% or more of shareholders voting Yes to win. And with only 3%, we can learn and re-submit a stronger proposal next year. For better and for worse, there are many individuals and institutions who own over 3% of Twitter.

Say “Yes” to exploring our options

We’re calling on major shareholders who believe in Twitter’s future to declare they'll vote Yes.

Declaring your Yes vote will attract a wave of positive press, other people considering a Yes vote, more press, more people, and so on.
